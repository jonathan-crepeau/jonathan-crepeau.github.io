I am an aspiring web developer with 10+ years of previous experience building direct hiring pipelines and partnerships for corporate companies, managing teams at rapidly growing small businesses, and driving measurable results as a college instructor through cross-team collaboration and strong communication. I am currently pursuing a career transition to leverage my extensive customer support & service experience, front-end web development skills, and instructional and troubleshooting strengths to find a position in either customer success engineering or frontend development.

My interest in tech was sparked during my early career in the education sector. I witnessed firsthand how technology can become a barrier rather than a tool of access when I worked as a College Counselor with high school seniors in the New York City public school system. Students discovered a maze of poorly designed websites and application portals when applying for city, state, or federal financial aid grants or scholarships. Ever since, the end user experience has been my ‘North Star’ in how I approach web development and front-end design.
